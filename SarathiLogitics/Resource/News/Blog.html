Sarathi Logistics Launch: Modernizing Transportation Across Nepal

24 July, 2025 By Sarathi Logistics Team

Headquartered in Swayambhu, Kathmandu, Sarathi Logistics Pvt. Ltd. has officially started operations from 15 locations including Narayanghat, Birgunj, Biratnagar, Butwal, Bhairahawa, Hetauda, Pokhara, Itahari, and Dhangadhi. This nationwide network ensures fast, reliable, and efficient logistics solutions for businesses and individuals across Nepal.

Sarathi specializes in Partial Truck Load (PTL) and Full Truck Load (FTL) services, along with courier and parcel delivery, fleet management, warehousing, and reverse logistics. Customers can easily book trucks, track shipments in real-time, and experience transparent service through our mobile app and website.

"Sarathi was established to make transportation in Nepal technical, transparent, and seamless," says Raj Kumar Neupane, Founder and CEO.

Sarathi is investing in modern warehouse management, GPS-enabled tracking, and AI-driven route optimization. These initiatives reduce delivery times, operational costs, and carbon footprint, making logistics sustainable and efficient.

Our customer-centric approach focuses on reliability, speed, and transparency. Trained personnel ensure safe handling of goods, timely deliveries, and exceptional support. Whether small parcels or large cargo, Sarathi provides tailor-made solutions.

Why Choose Sarathi Logistics?

  • Nationwide Coverage: Operations across all major cities ensure timely deliveries anywhere in Nepal.
  • Technology-Driven Solutions: Real-time tracking, route optimization, and digital booking make shipping simple.
  • Customer-Centric Service: Dedicated support team and transparent pricing build lasting trust.
  • Environmentally Conscious: Sustainable practices reduce the carbon footprint of transportation.
  • Experienced Team: Decades of experience in logistics, IT, and operations guarantee reliability and efficiency.

As Nepal's logistics landscape evolves, Sarathi Logistics remains committed to empowering businesses, connecting communities, and driving economic growth through innovative, reliable, and transparent transportation solutions.
