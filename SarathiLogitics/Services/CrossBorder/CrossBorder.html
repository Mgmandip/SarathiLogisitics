Cross Border Logistics

Cross-Border Logistics refers to the transportation and delivery of goods between countries, involving customs clearance, international shipping regulations, and multi-modal transport systems. At Sarathi Logistics, our Cross-Border service helps businesses in Nepal ship and receive goods across international borders, particularly with key trade partners like India, China, and other global markets. We handle the entire process from documentation and customs clearance to cross-border transportation and last-mile delivery. Our experienced cross-border logistics team ensures smooth, compliant, and timely movement of goods, helping Nepali businesses grow beyond national boundaries with confidence.
