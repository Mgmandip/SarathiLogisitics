About Sarathi

About

"Move the Nation, Deliver the Future"

Sarathi Logistics Pvt. Ltd. is a fast-growing logistics and cargo company headquartered in Kathmandu, Nepal. With an expanding network of branches across major cities including Biratnagar, Itahari, Birgunj, Hetauda, Narayanghat, Pokhara, Bhairahawa, Butwal, Dang and Dhangadhi, we are building a strong nationwide presence to serve businesses and communities effectively.

We specialize in Partial Truck Load (PTL), Full Truck Load (FTL), and Fulfillment Services, while steadily expanding into warehouse management, courier solutions, and digital logistics platforms. Our focus is on providing reliable, transparent, and customer-centric logistics solutions that enable seamless connectivity and efficiency across Nepal.

Our Vision

To be Nepal’s most trusted and transparent logistics partner, using technology to connect businesses and communities nationwide.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, transparent, and customer-focused logistics solutions through technology and operational excellence empowering trade, driving economic growth, and enhancing nationwide connectivity.
