Part Truckload (PTL)

Part Truckload is a shipping service designed for businesses that need to transport goods that are too large for parcel delivery but not enough to fill an entire truck. Instead of booking a full truck, your shipment shares space with others heading in the same direction—making it a more affordable and flexible option. At Sarathi Logistics, our PTL service helps small and mid-sized businesses ship goods across Nepal without paying for unused truck space. We consolidate multiple shipments, ensuring timely, safe, and cost-efficient delivery from city to city. With our optimized routing, professional handling, and transparent pricing, Sarathi’s PTL service is the smart choice for businesses that need flexibility, reliability, and affordability in their logistics.
