Express Parcel

"Dedicated. Reliable. Nationwide for Businesses."

At Sarathi Logistics, our Express Parcel service ensures time-sensitive shipments like online orders, business documents, electronics, or personal parcels are picked up and delivered within 24 to 72 hours across Nepal. Whether it's Kathmandu, Pokhara, Biratnagar, or remote districts, we offer door-to-door service with real-time tracking. Our strong delivery network, GPS-enabled fleet, and customer-focused operations make us the ideal partner for businesses and individuals looking for speed, reliability, and transparency in parcel shipping.
