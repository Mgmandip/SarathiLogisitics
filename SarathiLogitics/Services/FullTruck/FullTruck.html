Full Truck Load (FTL) Service

"Dedicated. Reliable. Nationwide for Businesses."

For businesses that need to move bulk cargo or large shipments securely and on time, Sarathi Full Truck Load (FTL) service provides a dedicated truck exclusively for your goods. With guaranteed capacity, faster transit, and professional handling, our FTL service is the ideal solution for businesses requiring efficiency, reliability, and control in nationwide logistics.

How It Works

Business Booking

Place your FTL order through our booking platform or with our FTL logistics team.

Truck Assignment

A dedicated vehicle is allocated based on cargo type, route, and urgency.

Safe Loading & Dispatch

Cargo is secured and dispatched directly to your destination.

Nationwide Transit

Direct movement with minimized stops for faster delivery.

Delivery Confirmation

Real-time tracking and proof of delivery upon arrival.
